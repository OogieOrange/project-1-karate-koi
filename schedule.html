Open House


If you, your child or even a friend is intrested in joining out club, we encourage you to visit our dojo on one of our open houses!

During open house hours, some members will be pressent and actively showing small demonstrations of basic karate. You will have a chance to ask any question you might have about our training or karate, and we will do our best to answer them. As well as have access to view our training facilities. We offer coffee or lemonade accompanied by a cinnamon bun to any who might want them.
