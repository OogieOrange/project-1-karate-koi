Why Karate?


Karate is, like many other sports, beneficial for the body. The training helps develop agility, strength and speed. While also improving muscle tone and endurance. Doing karate means you work with both the hands and feet, left and right sides equally. Doing this promotes balance and inevitably increase mental and physical coordination, as well as flexibility. Training in general can also help in developing more confidence and self-esteem in all parts of life.

What can seperate karate from for example footbal is the disceplined and respectful 'etiquette' that is encouraged towards instructors and fellow students alike. This teaches us to apply a good character even outside of training. The self-discepline that is inevitably developed through training also contributes to a certain restrain and self-control that can be applied outside the dojo.

The way we treat others in training translates into how we treat others and ourselves in the real world.

Open House

You will have a chance to ask any question you might have about our training or karate, and we will do our best to answer them. As well as have access to view our training facilities.
